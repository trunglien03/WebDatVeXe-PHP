Book Malaysia & Singapore Bus Tickets Online

Booking Malaysia bus tickets online and Singapore bus tickets online at BusOnlineTicket.com is easy, fast and secure. Besides, you get to save the time and cost to go to the bus terminal or bus station just to buy the tickets.

Browse through the bus schedule, check tickets availability and ticket price with our mobile apps and our mobile-friendly website. Our online system offers a 24/7 online bus booking service and you can secure your tickets in advance to ensure a smooth and well-planned trip.

Only Need 5 Minutes to Book Bus Tickets Online

Online bus ticket booking is so much easier than what you think.

You only need to select your origin, destinations, number of pax and departure date and we will provide you with the list of available trips for booking.

Next, select your preferred bus operators, choose the seat and proceed to the next step.

In the next page, you will have to fill in the passengers details. Choose your preferred payment option and proceed to make the payment.

Once the payment is confirmed, we will send your booking confirmation to your email within 24 hours.
